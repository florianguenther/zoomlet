zoomlet by florianguenther

A bookmarklet to turn every website into a zoomable canvas.

Usage

  • Bookmark this link: Zoomlet (drag it to your bookmark bar)
  • Visit any website you want
  • Click the bookmarklet
  • Use your Mouswheel to zoom in and out, click and hold to pan around
  • ... or - on an iPad - use two fingers to zoom and pan

The Bookmarklet-Link Code

javascript:(function(){
  document.body.appendChild(document.createElement('script'))
  .src='http://florianguenther.com/zoomlet/assets/zoomlet.js';
})();
